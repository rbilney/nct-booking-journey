Join us - and each other

Being the UK’s largest charity for parents also means we’re the UK’s largest community of new parents.

By becoming NCT members, thousands of parents have formed lifelong friendships and support networks. Through us, they’ve shared experiences, excitement and inspiration during some of the most amazing times of their lives – and beyond.

Your membership donation will support our work in your community, and in local communities across the UK.

What's included

Our NCT Members club gives you a wide range of money-saving rewards, discounts and services – from the weekly shop to maternity clothes, from date-night cinema tickets to laptops, and household bills to holidays (with or without the kids).

Take a peek at what we offer:

  • priority entry and bags of bargains at our famous countrywide NCT Nearly New Sales
  • a calendar full of events and new parent meet-ups at your local NCT branch
  • a free subscription to NCT Matters, our inspiring and informative digital magazine.

What you’re giving

By supporting us, we can always be there for parents up and down the country – providing practical information, emotional support and a strong voice in our society.

Your membership donation helps us:

  • provide support services such as our helpline and our independent, online pregnancy, birth and parenting information centre
  • provide local outreach services across the country, helping new families in disadvantaged communities get the best start in life
  • campaign on behalf of parents to change the things that matter to them.
